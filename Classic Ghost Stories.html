CLASSIC GHOST STORIES

Book info:

Classic ghost stories is a compilation of classic horror tales written by various authors, such as "Charles Dickens", "Edith Nesbit" or "Robert Lewis Stevenson".
It contains adapted versions of: "The signal-man", "The marble finger" and "The body snatcher".
This edition of the book also comes with various activities for pupils to do after the reading.
